About

Aaro Nuoramo

Hi! I'm Aaro Nuoramo, a 20-year-old student from Jyväskylä, Finland. I am currently in my first year studying for a degree in information and communication technology at Jyväskylä University of Applied Sciences. My goal in my studies is to specialise in cyber security and graduate within four years. In my freetime I train cycling, work as a freelance photographer and drink special coffees.


I have been training cycling for about four years now and I have raced in mountainbike XCO and XCM. I have also cycled 290 km in Saimaa Cycle Tour cycling event as the youngest participant in 2021. I cycled the whole 290 km in just under 12 hours. My cycling hobby got me a summer job as a bike mechanic at Pyörävarikko Imatra where I was responsible for bike repairs in the 2023 cycling season.


I have been working as a freelance photograher in addition to studies since 2018. My photography "career" got wind under it when I was doing one week long work practice program at my local Ice hockey team Imatran Ketterä's media team. After the one week period was over thei asked me over and over again to take photos of the home matches and suddenly I became their team photographer. At some point, I started getting paid for photography, so my hobby became a job. Since then, I have been doing photography work alongside my studies. I started working as a photographer through my father's company because it was easier when I was underage and I could learn about entrepreneurship. In addition to ice hockey, I have done photo and video work for many other sports clubs and local businesses in South Karelia.
Check out my photography website (in Finnish): www.elitpro.fi
